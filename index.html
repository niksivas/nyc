NEW YORK CITY


Travel and Tourism




New York City (NYC) fondly known as the "Big Apple", is a bustling metropolis situated in the state of New York, United States of America (U.S.A) and one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. As a melting pot of culture, finance, trade and art, New York City has something for every style, taste and budget.

WEATHER







What are the best times to travel to NYC?

The weather is usually warmer during the months of June to September.
The colder weather usually occurs between the months of December to March.

ACCOMODATION







What are the different choices of AirBnb's available in the five boroughs?



The wide variety of room types available in New York City, allows visitors to have their pick in accomodation. In Manhattan, usually entire apartments are rented out to give privacy to their guests.


Furthermore, private rooms are mostly rented out in Brooklyn, compared to the other boroughs.

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS







What is a 'must-visit' place to check out in NYC?



The best Farmers' Markets should not be missed as the five NYC Boroughs have a varied range of organic food produce. Of all the boroughs, Brooklyn has more markets available, most of which are open on Saturdays between 8 a.m to 3 p.m.




SAFETY







Overall, is New York City safe for tourists?



New York state is generally safe for tourists as the average hate crime rate per 100,000 of the population is 3.1 which is lower compared to many other U.S.A states such as Massachusetts. Thus, this is reassuring the personal safety and wellbeing of tourists in the city.
